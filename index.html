13.Bourgeois Life – Bürgerliches Leben

In 1905 Theodor Weyler wrote in his guide to converse in the family, public life and good society: “The study should be furnished with bookcases, shelves, a comfortable armchair, several good pictures, weapons and busts. It must have a serious, dignified character, but also create a comfortable impression.” This Wolfenbüttel study from the early 20th century perfectly embodies that description.
